Semantic Scholar

AI-Powered Academic Metadata, Citation Networks, and Impact Metrics

Home › Databases › Semantic Scholar

Overview

Semantic Scholar is an AI-powered academic search engine that automatically extracts information from research papers across all scientific disciplines. It provides intelligent metadata extraction, citation networks, influence metrics, and paper recommendations powered by machine learning. The platform indexes over 200 million research papers from diverse sources including PubMed, arXiv, and open access repositories.

Intelligent Discovery — Semantic Scholar uses AI to extract key entities (genes, proteins, methods) from paper text, build citation networks, and identify influential papers based on semantic similarity and citation impact.

Key Features

  • AI-powered entity extraction from papers
  • Citation relationship mapping
  • Influence and impact metrics
  • Semantic paper similarity
  • Author profile and impact tracking
  • Institution and research collaboration analysis

Use Cases

  • Find influential papers in a research area
  • Discover citation networks and research lineages
  • Search for specific methods or techniques
  • Track author impact and collaboration
  • Identify trending research topics
  • Find similar papers and research directions

AI-Powered Paper Search and Analysis

semantic_scholar_search

Search Semantic Scholar for papers with AI-powered analysis and citation metrics.

Parameters: query (string: keywords, topics, or natural language), author (string, optional: author name), year (integer, optional: publication year or range)

Returns: Paper metadata, citation counts, influence score, extracted entities, similar papers, author information, open access links

Search Modifiers:

  • query="BRCA1 AND mutations" — Boolean operators for precision
  • author="Smith, Jane" — Find papers by specific author
  • year=2023 — Filter by publication year
  • query="CRISPR gene therapy" author="Zhang" — Combined search

Example Queries

Find influential papers on a topic

"Show me the most influential papers on CRISPR gene therapy from the last 5 years."

Tool: semantic_scholar_search with query="CRISPR gene therapy", year=2019-2024

Discover papers by an author

"Find all papers by Jennifer Doudna related to CRISPR biology."

Tool: semantic_scholar_search with query="CRISPR", author="Doudna, Jennifer"

Explore citation networks

"What papers cite the seminal BRCA1 discovery paper, and what research directions have they explored?"

Tool: semantic_scholar_search with query="BRCA1" to identify foundational work and citations

Track research trends

"What are the trending topics in single-cell genomics, and who are the leading researchers?"

Tool: semantic_scholar_search with query="single-cell genomics" filtered by recent years

Search Strategies

Semantic Search

Semantic Scholar understands natural language queries, allowing searches like "DNA repair mechanisms" to find papers about various repair pathways without explicit keyword matching.

Citation Network Analysis

  • Identify landmark papers and citations
  • Trace research lineages and methodological development
  • Discover research communities and collaborations

Entity-Based Discovery

Search for papers discussing specific genes, proteins, diseases, or methods thanks to AI entity extraction. Find papers that mention your genes of interest even if they aren't in the abstract.

Author and Impact Tracking

Monitor authors, research groups, and institutions for publication activity and influence metrics.

Integration with Datasets MCP

Semantic Scholar enhances literature discovery workflows:

  • With PubMed: Combine abstract search with semantic paper recommendations
  • With PMC: Find influential papers, then access full text
  • With Gene Databases: Discover papers about specific genes ranked by influence
  • With UniProt: Find protein-centric research and citation contexts
