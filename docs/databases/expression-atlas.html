Expression Atlas

Gene Expression Experiments, Baseline Expression, and Disease Differential Expression

Home › Databases › Expression Atlas

Overview

Expression Atlas is a resource for gene expression data across multiple organisms and experimental conditions. It provides access to baseline expression patterns across tissues and cell types, and differential expression analysis in disease states, developmental stages, and experimental treatments. Data comes from RNA-seq and microarray experiments submitted to ArrayExpress and the ENA.

Expression Insights — Expression Atlas integrates thousands of curated experiments to provide tissue-specific expression profiles, developmental expression patterns, and disease-associated expression changes.

Key Features

  • Baseline gene expression across tissues
  • Tissue-specific expression patterns
  • Differential expression in diseases
  • Developmental expression timelines
  • Experimental condition comparisons
  • Multi-organism expression data

Use Cases

  • Find which tissues express a gene
  • Identify disease-related expression changes
  • Compare expression across conditions
  • Discover tissue-specific splice variants
  • Analyze developmental expression patterns
  • Investigate treatment responses

Gene Expression Search and Analysis

expression_atlas_search

Query gene expression data across tissues, conditions, and disease states.

Parameters: gene (string: gene symbol or Ensembl ID), organism (string, optional: species name), experiment (string, optional: experiment type or condition)

Returns: Expression values, tissue specificity, fold-changes, p-values, experiment metadata, expression plots

Experiment Types:

  • baseline — Normal tissue expression across cell types
  • differential — Disease vs. normal comparison
  • developmental — Expression during development
  • treatment — Drug or stimulus response
  • single-cell — Single-cell RNA-seq data
  • spatial — Spatial transcriptomics data

Example Queries

Find tissue-specific expression

"Which tissues express the BRCA1 gene at high levels?"

Tool: expression_atlas_search with gene="BRCA1"

Identify disease-related expression changes

"How is TP53 expression altered in cancer samples compared to normal tissue?"

Tool: expression_atlas_search with gene="TP53", experiment="differential"

Analyze developmental expression

"What is the expression pattern of SOX2 during human neural development?"

Tool: expression_atlas_search with gene="SOX2", experiment="developmental"

Compare expression across conditions

"How does CFTR expression change in response to hypoxia treatment?"

Tool: expression_atlas_search with gene="CFTR", experiment="treatment"

Search Strategies

Baseline Expression Analysis

Retrieve normal expression levels across 60+ tissues and cell types to understand which tissues express your gene of interest and identify tissue-specific expression patterns.

Disease Expression Profiling

  • Compare expression in disease samples vs. healthy controls
  • Identify significantly dysregulated genes
  • Find disease biomarkers and prognostic markers

Single-Cell Resolution

Access single-cell RNA-seq experiments to determine expression at cell-type and developmental stage-specific resolution.

Multi-Organism Comparative Analysis

Compare expression patterns between human, mouse, zebrafish, and other organisms to understand evolutionary conservation of tissue-specific expression.

Integration with Datasets MCP

Expression Atlas enhances functional genomics research:

  • With Ensembl: Map genes to genomic coordinates and retrieve transcript-specific expression
  • With GTEx: Compare tissue expression across databases
  • With UniProt: Link expression patterns to protein localization and function
  • With Reactome: Analyze genes in disease-specific pathways
