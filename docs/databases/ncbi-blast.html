NCBI BLAST

Sequence Similarity Search Against NCBI Nucleotide and Protein Databases

Home › Databases › NCBI BLAST

Overview

NCBI BLAST (Basic Local Alignment Search Tool) is the gold standard for sequence similarity searching. It compares a DNA, RNA, or protein sequence against vast databases to find similar sequences, identifying homologs, orthologs, and potential off-target matches.

Multiple BLAST Variants Available: blastn (nucleotide-nucleotide), blastp (protein-protein), blastx (translated DNA search), tblastn (protein against translated DNA), and more.

Key Features

  • Nucleotide (blastn) searches
  • Protein (blastp) searches
  • Translated searches (blastx, tblastn)
  • Optimized scoring matrices (BLOSUM, PAM)
  • E-value statistics
  • Multi-hit alignment reporting

Use Cases

  • Find homologous sequences
  • Identify gene origin/organism
  • Detect potential splice variants
  • Screen for off-target amplification
  • Validate sequence assemblies
  • Discover evolutionary relationships

Search Targets Available

Database Description Program
Nucleotide (nr) All non-redundant nucleotide sequences blastn, tblastn
Protein (nr) All non-redundant protein sequences blastp, tblastn
RefSeq NCBI curated reference sequences blastn, blastp
UniProt Universal Protein Resource sequences blastp
Genome Assemblies Specific organism genome databases blastn, blastx
EST (GSS) Expressed sequence tags and genome survey sequences blastn

BLAST Search Operations

blast_search

Perform a BLAST search against NCBI databases.

Parameters:

  • sequence (string) - DNA, RNA, or protein sequence
  • program (string) - blastn, blastp, blastx, tblastn, tblastx
  • database (string, optional) - nr, refseq, or genome-specific
  • max_hits (integer, optional) - limit results (default: 100)
  • evalue_threshold (float, optional) - E-value cutoff (default: 1e-5)

Returns: Ranked hits with scores, E-values, alignments, and metadata

Example Queries

Find homologous genes

"Search for sequences similar to the human EGFR gene. Show the top 10 matches."

Tool: blast_search with program="blastn", max_hits=10

Verify PCR primer specificity

"BLAST this PCR primer sequence against the human genome to check for off-target binding: CGGTGCTGGTCTGGGTCTG"

Tool: blast_search with program="blastn"

Protein-protein homology

"Find protein homologs to the TP53 protein sequence in the nr database."

Tool: blast_search with program="blastp"

Scoring & Statistics

BLAST results are ranked by statistical significance using E-values (expected values). A lower E-value indicates greater statistical significance of the match.

E-value Interpretation:
  • E < 1e-10: Extremely significant (near-certain match)
  • E < 1e-5: Highly significant (very likely homolog)
  • E < 0.01: Significant (probable homolog)
  • E > 0.01: Marginal (weak similarity)
