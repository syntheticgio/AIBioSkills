NCBI PubMed

Biomedical Literature Database with Search, Abstracts, and Citation Data

Home › Databases › NCBI PubMed

Overview

PubMed is a free search interface accessing the MEDLINE database of references on life sciences and biomedical information. It comprises over 36 million citations from MEDLINE, life science journals, and online books, with abstracts and article metadata. PubMed is the primary literature resource for bioinformatics research discovery and knowledge synthesis.

Comprehensive Coverage — PubMed indexes journals from 1960s onward and includes full-text availability links, MeSH annotations, and author/institution metadata for advanced research discovery.

Key Features

  • Full-text literature search with Boolean operators
  • Abstract retrieval with metadata
  • Author and journal filtering
  • MeSH term indexing and annotation
  • Citation/reference tracking
  • Full-text article links

Use Cases

  • Literature review on a gene or protein
  • Find papers about a disease association
  • Search for clinical trial results
  • Identify all papers by an author
  • Track citations for a topic
  • Discover recent methodological papers

Literature Search

pubmed_search

Search PubMed literature by keyword, gene, or topic.

Parameters: query (string: keywords, gene symbols, or Boolean operators), max_results (integer, optional: default 10)

Returns: PMID, title, abstract, authors, journal, publication year, full-text links

Query Syntax Examples:

  • BRCA1 AND cancer — Genes with AND/OR/NOT operators
  • "familial hypercholesterolemia" 2020:2024[pdat] — Quoted phrases and date filtering
  • gene_symbol[GENE] — Gene-specific search
  • meslek[MAJR] — Major MeSH terms only

Example Queries

Literature review on a gene

"Find recent papers about BRCA1 mutations and breast cancer risk."

Tool: pubmed_search with query="BRCA1 AND breast cancer"

Find clinical trial results

"Search PubMed for clinical trials on CRISPR gene therapy published in the last 3 years."

Tool: pubmed_search with query="CRISPR AND gene therapy AND clinical trial"

Author and methodology search

"Find papers by Jane Smith on machine learning in genomics."

Tool: pubmed_search with query="Smith[au] AND machine learning AND genomics"

Disease-gene association discovery

"What papers discuss the TP53 gene and neuroblastoma?"

Tool: pubmed_search with query="TP53[GENE] AND neuroblastoma"

Search Strategies

Using MeSH Terms

PubMed uses Medical Subject Headings (MeSH) for standardized indexing. Search with [MAJR] for major topics or [MESH] for all MeSH-indexed articles.

Field Tags

  • [TIAB] — Title and abstract
  • [AU] — Author name
  • [GENE] — Gene symbol
  • [PDAT] — Publication date
  • [PT] — Publication type (e.g., Journal Article, Review)
  • [LA] — Language

Date Ranges

Use format YYYY:YYYY[PDAT] to restrict results to a date range. Example: 2020:2024[pdat] for articles published 2020-2024.
